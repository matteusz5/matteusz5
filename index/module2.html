Module 2: Graphics and Media Integration

In this module, you'll explore how to insert and manipulate graphics, shapes, icons, and media files in Microsoft Word to enhance the visual appeal of your documents.

Topics Covered:

  • Inserting Pictures and Icons
  • Using Shapes to Enhance Design
  • Embedding Videos

Practical Exercise:

Create a marketing flyer with pictures, icons, and a promotional video embedded to illustrate a product's features.
